Hi there! Let's start with downloading your planner.

1.  

After clicking the link to your planner, you will then be routed to the Dropbox website. If you do not want to get the Dropbox app you can click on "Or continue to website".

2.  

After clicking "Or continue to website", it will still ask you to sign up but you do not have to, unless you want to create an account. Please exit out this box and it will show you the planner and the option to download it.

3.  

Once you click on the "Download" button on the top left hand side corner of the screen, your planner will start downloading.

4.  

The image above will show you the screen after clicking the download button.

5.  

After the download, click the hyperlink next to it "Open in..." and click the option "Open in GoodNotes".

6.  

Then select which folder you would like to import your planner to.

7.  

Then select "Import as New Document".

8.  

You are now ready to use your planner!.
